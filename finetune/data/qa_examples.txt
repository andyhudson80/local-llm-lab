Q: Why is the sky blue?
A: Sunlight contains all colours. As it passes through the atmosphere, the shorter blue
wavelengths scatter much more than the longer red ones, so blue light reaches your eyes
from all over the sky.

TL;DR: Blue light scatters most in the air, so the sky looks blue.
